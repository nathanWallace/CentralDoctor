Dr Charles Murray

MA PhD MRCP

Royal Free NHS Trust

Dr Murray is a Consultant Physician and Gastroenterologist at the Royal Free NHS Trust where he is also Clinical Lead for Gastrointestinal Endoscopy.


Dr Murray undertook his initial medical studies at Cambridge University, later qualifying with distinction from the University of London. Between 2001 and 2005 he conducted his initial research period at St Mark’s Hospital, concentrating on both basic and clinical science, leading to the award of a PhD in 2006 from Imperial College London.


Dr Murray’s clinical practice encompasses all aspects of gastroenterology with specific interests in endoscopy, inflammatory bowel disease, functional gut disorders including IBS, and nutrition and obesity.


For his work to date, Dr Murray was awarded the Moti L. & Kamla Rustgi International Fellowship by the American Gastroenterological Association in 2005 and in 2007 was the Functional Brain-Gut Research Group Young Investigator of the Year. He is currently Secretary of the British Society of Gastroenterology.
